Project principles

Careful by design.

A bakery system should be dependable enough to disappear into the work.

  1. 01

    Exact formulas

    Source weights, yields, timing, temperatures, and chemistry remain protected.

  2. 02

    Private by default

    Operational systems and business records stay off the public internet.

  3. 03

    Confirm consequential writes

    Purchasing, stock, payment, and data changes are previewed before they happen.

  4. 04

    Evidence over optimism

    Work is complete when it survives verification and real operations—not when a feature merely exists.
